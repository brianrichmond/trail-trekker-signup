Sign-up Sheet Tool

El Cerrito Trail Trekkers

This tool reads a photo or scan of a paper sign-up sheet and converts it into a spreadsheet file you can import into Mailchimp.

1

Set up your API key (first time only)

The tool uses an AI service to read handwriting. You'll need an API key — contact your Trail Trekkers coordinator to get one, or create your own free account at console.anthropic.com.

Once you have a key, paste it into the app and click Save key. You won't need to enter it again on this device.

2

Take a photo of the sign-up sheet

Good lighting and a straight-on angle give the best results. The sheet should fill most of the frame.

iPhone tip: Go to Settings → Camera → Formats → select Most Compatible. Do this once and your photos will always upload correctly. If you've already taken a photo in the old format, open it in Photos, tap Share, and choose Save to Files — this converts it automatically.
3

Upload and download

Open the app, drag your photo or PDF into the upload area, and click Extract & build CSV. Review the preview to spot any errors, then click Download CSV.

The CSV is ready to import directly into Mailchimp or open in Excel or Google Sheets.
